🛡️ How to Stay Safe from Fake Jobs
💸Never pay any fee to get a job — registration, training or equipment fees are classic scam tactics.
📧Legitimate companies always provide official domain emails, not Gmail or Yahoo.
📱Avoid jobs that only contact via WhatsApp or Telegram — real recruiters use official channels.
🔍Google the company name + "scam" or "review" before applying.
💰Salaries of Rs 500–1000 per day for simple home tasks are almost always fake.
